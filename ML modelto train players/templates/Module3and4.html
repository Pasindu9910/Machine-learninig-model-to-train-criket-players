MODULE 3 AND 4

Training Focus: Batting technique, consistency, mental preparation,Speed, power hitting, shot selection.
Training Modules:
   Batting Technique:Refine shot execution and shot selection for consistent run-scoring.
   Mental Preparation: Develop concentration and focus techniques to maintain consistency.
   Video Analysis:Study own batting footage to identify and rectify technical flaws.
  Speed Work:Improve running between the wickets and reaction time.
  Power Hitting Training: Develop the ability to clear the boundary.
  Shot Repertoire:Expand range of shots to find scoring opportunities.

Batting Technique:
  Technical Drills:
    Practice forward defensive shots, backfoot punches, and cover drives in the nets.
    Focus on correct grip, stance, and footwork.
Consistency:
  Target Practice:
    Set up cones or markers in the nets as targets.
    Aim to hit the targets consistently with various shots for 15 minutes.
Mental Preparation:
  Visualization Exercise:
    Spend 10 minutes visualizing successful batting scenarios, focusing on positive outcomes and confident mindset.
Speed:
  Interval Sprints:
    Sprint at maximum effort for 30 seconds, followed by 1 minute of rest.
    Repeat for 10 intervals.
Power Hitting:
  Medicine Ball Slams:
    Perform 3 sets of 12 medicine ball slams, focusing on explosive power.
    Rest for 1 minute between sets.
Shot Selection:
  Match Scenario Drills:
    Practice shot selection based on different match scenarios (e.g., powerplay, death overs).
    Focus on choosing the right shot for the situation.
