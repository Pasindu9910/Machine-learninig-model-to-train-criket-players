MODULE 1,2 AND 4

Training Focus:Core strength, upper body strength, lower body strength, Endurance, concentration, footwork, Speed, power hitting, shot selection
Training Modules:
   Core Strength Exercises:Enhance stability and balance for prolonged batting stints.
   Upper Body and Lower Body Strength Exercises: Aid in generating power for boundary-hitting shots and running between the wickets.
   Endurance Training:Build stamina for batting long innings and converting starts into substantial scores.
  Concentration and Focus Techniques:Develop mental toughness to bat for extended periods.
  Footwork Drills: Enhance foot movement and positioning to face a high number of deliveries.
  Speed Work:Improve running between the wickets and reaction time.
  Power Hitting Training: Develop the ability to clear the boundary.
  Shot Repertoire:Expand range of shots to find scoring opportunities.

Core Strength:
  Plank Circuit:
    Perform 3 sets of planks for 30 seconds each, with 1-minute rest between sets.
    Include side planks and plank variations (e.g., plank leg lifts) for a balanced core workout.
Upper Body Strength:
  Push-up Pyramid:
    Perform sets of push-ups starting with 5 reps, then increasing by 1 rep each set until failure.
    Rest for 30 seconds between sets.
Lower Body Strength:
  Squat Jumps:
    Perform 3 sets of 10 squat jumps, focusing on explosive power.
    Rest for 1 minute between sets.
Endurance:
  Interval Runs:
    Run at a moderate pace for 2 minutes, followed by a 1-minute sprint.
    Repeat for 20 minutes.
Concentration:
  Focused Batting Drills:
    Practice hitting balls with a narrow focus on technique and shot selection.
    Visualize each shot before execution.
Footwork:
  Agility Ladder:
    Complete 3 sets of agility ladder drills, focusing on quick and precise footwork.
    Rest for 1 minute between sets.
Speed:
  Sprint Intervals:
    Sprint at maximum effort for 30 seconds, followed by 1 minute of rest.
    Repeat for 10 intervals.
Power Hitting:
  Medicine Ball Slams:
    Perform 3 sets of 12 medicine ball slams, focusing on explosive power.
    Rest for 1 minute between sets.
Shot Selection:
  Match Scenario Drills:
    Practice shot selection based on different match scenarios (e.g., powerplay, death overs).
    Focus on choosing the right shot for the situation.
