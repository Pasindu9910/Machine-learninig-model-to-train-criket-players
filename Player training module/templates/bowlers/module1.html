Technical Skills

Training Focus: Short Spell Efficiency,Medium Spell Consistency,
Long Spell Stamina,Tight Bowling,Controlled Aggression,Damage Limitation
Training Modules:
   Short Spell Efficiency:Focus on bowling short spells with high intensity, aiming for accuracy and maintaining a consistent line and length throughout.
   Medium Spell Consistency:Practice bowling medium-length spells with a focus on maintaining consistent line and length, while varying the pace and using subtle variations to deceive the batsman.
   Long Spell Stamina:Work on improving endurance and stamina through fitness training and bowling extended spells in nets, gradually increasing the number of overs bowled.
   Tight Bowling:Practice bowling economically by focusing on minimizing boundary balls, mixing up deliveries, and applying pressure on the batsman by maintaining a tight line and length.
