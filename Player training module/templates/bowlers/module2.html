Tactics and Strategy

Training Focus:Run Control Tactics,Field Placement Strategy,
Pressure Building,Boundary Control,Variation and Deception
Training Modules:
   Run Control Tactics:Develop tactics to control the flow of runs by varying the pace, line, and length according to the match situation, aiming to restrict the scoring opportunities.
   Field Placement Strategy:Learn to set up field placements strategically based on the batsman's weaknesses and the match situation to create pressure and induce mistakes.
   Pressure Building:Focus on building pressure on the batsman by bowling consistent lines and lengths, using attacking field placements, and maintaining a positive body language.
   Boundary Control: Develop strategies to minimize boundaries by bowling to defensive field settings, varying the pace, and bowling tight lines.
