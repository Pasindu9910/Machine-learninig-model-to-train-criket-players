Wicket-taking and Attack

Training Focus:Wicket-taking Techniques,Breakthrough Bowling,Advanced Wicket-taking,Speed and Swing,Defensive Bowling
Training Modules:
   Wicket-taking Techniques:Work on developing bowling variations such as swing, seam, and spin to create opportunities to take wickets.
   Breakthrough Bowling:Focus on delivering impactful spells aimed at breaking partnerships and taking crucial wickets at key moments of the game.
   Advanced Wicket-taking:Learn to set up batsmen with clever bowling tactics, such as using variations, changes in pace, and deceptive deliveries to outsmart them.
   Speed and Swing: Develop the ability to bowl at high speeds with control while also mastering swing bowling techniques to trouble the batsmen.
