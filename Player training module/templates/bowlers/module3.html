Precision and Consistency

Training Focus: Precision Bowling,Consistency Training,Dot Ball Maximization,Bowling Discipline
Training Modules:
   Precision Bowling:Focus on hitting specific areas consistently, such as off-stump or just outside off, with each delivery to force batsmen into making mistakes.
   Consistency Training:Work on repeating the same action consistently to ensure consistent bowling performance, focusing on rhythm and balance.
   Dot Ball Maximization:Aim to bowl a high percentage of dot balls by maintaining a consistent line and length, forcing the batsman to defend without scoring.
   Bowling Discipline: Train to maintain discipline in bowling plans and execution, adhering to the team strategy and bowling to set field placements.
